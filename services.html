Our Services

Comprehensive mental health and family support services tailored to your needs

What We Offer

Psychological Counselling

Individual therapy sessions designed to help you navigate life's challenges, manage stress, anxiety, depression, and other mental health concerns.

  • Initial assessment and evaluation
  • Personalized treatment plans
  • Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)
  • Solution-focused therapy
  • Online and in-person sessions
  • Follow-up and progress monitoring

Family Therapy

Strengthen family bonds and improve communication through specialized family therapy sessions that address relationship dynamics and conflicts.

  • Couples therapy and relationship counselling
  • Parenting support and guidance
  • Family mediation and conflict resolution
  • Blended family support
  • Teen and adolescent family therapy
  • Cultural and religious sensitivity

Social Support & Workshops

Community-based programs and educational workshops designed to promote mental health awareness and build stronger, more resilient communities.

  • Mental health awareness workshops
  • Stress management group sessions
  • Parenting skills training
  • Community support groups
  • Workplace mental health programs
  • Cultural integration support

Student Support

Specialized counselling services for students dealing with academic stress, social challenges, and life transitions.

  • Academic stress and exam anxiety
  • Social skills and peer relationships
  • Career guidance and decision making
  • University transition support
  • Study skills and time management
  • Bullying and peer pressure
