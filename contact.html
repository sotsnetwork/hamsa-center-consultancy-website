Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment by calling us at +971 4 2368822, emailing info@hamsaconsultancy.com, or filling out our contact form above.

Do you offer online sessions?

Yes, we offer both online and in-person sessions. You can choose the format that works best for you.

What insurance do you accept?

We accept Dubai Health Insurance, ADNIC, Oman Insurance, and Daman. Please contact us to verify your coverage.

How long are the sessions?

Individual sessions are 50 minutes, family sessions are 60 minutes, and workshops are typically 2 hours.
