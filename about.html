About Hamsa Consultancy

Dedicated to supporting mental health and family wellbeing in Dubai

Our Mission

To support individuals, families, and communities in their mental and social wellbeing through professional, compassionate, and culturally sensitive psychological services.

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Hamsa Consultancy began as a small practice with a big vision: to make mental health support accessible and culturally appropriate for the diverse community of Dubai.

Over the years, we have grown to become one of Dubai's most trusted psychological and family counselling centers, serving over 2,000 clients from various cultural backgrounds.

Our name 'Hamsa' represents the protective hand symbol that brings good fortune and protection - reflecting our commitment to providing a safe, supportive environment for healing and growth.

Our Values

Compassion

We maintain the highest ethical standards and professional conduct in all our interactions.
